Global Learning Services

Admission Guidance

At GLS, we assess the student's English language skills & his previous student profile.Then after analysing, we give our recommendations regarding the best suitable study course for the student. We then follow up with the selected authorities to help a speedy and positive response.

Colleges / Universities Selection

At GLS, we help students get admission into universities or colleges that best suit their personal, academic, financial and other parameters.

Study Visa Counseling

At GLS, we make sure that all possible efforts are taken to guide students in selecting their study programs and tell them in detail about their visa type,conditions and obligations.

Visa Assistance

The competent staff at GLS assists students with the visa applications, offer guidelines for financial statements and help students in preparing their file to meet the requirements of the High Commission to ensure 100% Visa success.

Finance Assistance

Money plays the most significant role in releasing your dreams for education. GLS offers guidance to students for education loans. At GLS, we have knowledge about all the national banks providing student loans at affordable rates and with various approved financial institutions and private banks offering loans that we share with our students and their family members.

Pre-Departure Guidance

At GLS, we organise pre departure training sessions for the students. It has been observed that majority of students traveling abroad face hassles with :- airport transit, immigration, landing, luggage etc. We at GLS explain each and every aspect of pre-departure to the student.
